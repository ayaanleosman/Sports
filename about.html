Our mission at SportsDaily is to make sure sports fans are up to date with sports the quickest way possible and not to waste their time. we offer hard-hitting news stories, summarised so the viewers do not miss anything with our bitesized stories. We also like to incorporate videos and images to tell a story because words alone can be a bore at times and images and videos can tell stories better than words at times. Many sports sites have more than 20 sports that they cover but we just focus on 3 and make sure we cover everything. we would want be the go-to website for Football, Boxing and Basketball rather than be mediocre but cover many sports.
